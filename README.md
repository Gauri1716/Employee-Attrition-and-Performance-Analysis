#  Employee Attrition and Performance Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition and performance across departments. The dashboard provides HR professionals and business managers with insights into workforce trends, employee turnover, and performance metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

----------------------

## Dashboard Pages

### Landing Page
- Navigation to different dashboards
- User-friendly report interface

### Employee Attrition Dashboard
- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Inactive Employees
- Overall Attrition Rate
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Education Field

### Employee Performance Dashboard
- Total Employees
- Average Monthly Income
- Average Performance Rating
- Average Job Satisfaction
- Performance Rating by Department
- Performance by Job Role
- Performance by Overtime
- Performance by Marital Status

##  Dataset

The dashboard uses an employee HR dataset containing information such as:

- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Education Field
- Monthly Income
- Performance Rating
- Job Satisfaction
- Attrition
- Overtime
- Marital Status

-------------------------

##  Business Insights

- Identify departments with high employee attrition.
- Analyze attrition trends across job roles and education fields.
- Compare employee performance among departments.
- Understand the relationship between overtime and performance.
- Monitor employee satisfaction and workforce health.
